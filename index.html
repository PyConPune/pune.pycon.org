PyCon Pune 2018

February 8 - 11, 2018

About

PyCon, the gathering for the community using and developing the open-source Python programming language. During PyCon Pune, the community will meet for two days of talks and work on upstream projects in two days of dev sprint.

Getting Involved

PyCon Pune is a community event and is volunteer-driven. People from the community come forward to volunteer and help make the conference better. Anybody attending PyCon Pune is welcome to volunteer, but you must be registered to volunteer.

If you are looking to volunteer, drop us an email.

Sponsors

PyCon Pune is entirely driven by volunteer. Sponsoring the event helps to sustain, grow the conference along with the community. Sponsors help to make the conference affordable for community, and maintaining the inventory for the conference.
